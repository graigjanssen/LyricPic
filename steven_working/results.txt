The song climbed to #1 in 20 countries, racking up over 7 million downloads to make it Gaga’s best selling digital single. The track won Gaga her first Grammy for <a href="https://www.grammy.com/artist/lady-gaga" rel="nofollow">Best Dance Recording</a> at the 52nd Grammy Awards, while it was also nominated for Song of the Year and Record of the Year.</p>
